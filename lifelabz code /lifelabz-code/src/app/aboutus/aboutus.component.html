Our Story

Our story begins in 2016 with the establishment of Life9 Systems to provide solutions and services in the digital arena. In 2019, we transformed into an IoT solutions provider committed to empowering organizations to transform digitally, across verticals. Our team brings decades of experience in smart device design to LifeLabz.

LifeLabz is our product endeavor with a vision to help bring smart life solutions to every aspect of our individual and collective lives.
